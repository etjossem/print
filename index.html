This Changes Everything

Here at SendGrid, we're always keeping an eye on new ways to help you communicate with your customers. So when a publically traded innovator in our space announced they'd built a platform for sending programmable faxes, we knew we had to step up our game and deliver something truly special. Meet Print.

Print is a protocol for receiving ink via networked transmission. When you use SendGrid as your fax API, your recipients can activate Print with the touch of a button. They'll be prompted to select a compatible fax machine (or dedicated printing hardware, sold separately) on their network and choose how many hardcopies they want. Print has undergone years of beta testing, and it's natively supported by almost every browser, including the one you're using right now.
